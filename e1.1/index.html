E1.1

ScienceLab is a modular experimental system for physics, chemistry and biology. This modern student experiment system can also be used at vocational schools.

We recommend a look at our Science Lab program of the natural sciences.

The ScienceLab consists of several components:

  • Test-specific devices
  • Device-shaped storage, optionally block or block
  • Exercise literature
  • General, system-independent accessories

This system concept makes it possible to adapt to the most diverse requirements through regional and national curricula, textbooks, age levels and training levels.
