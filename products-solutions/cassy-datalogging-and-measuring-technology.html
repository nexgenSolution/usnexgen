Measurement data acquisition and evaluation

for all requirements and all levels of education

More than 30 years ago, the idea was born to develop a modular and compatible system of measuring instruments, sensor boxes and sensors for various measuring capabilities at schools, vocational training colleges and universities. This resulted in the CASSY system.

Today, the CASSY family is one of the most successful and popular systems for measurement data acquisition and evaluation worldwide.

The modular system can be used in the natural sciences, physics, chemistry and biology as well as in vocational training.

The CASSY System

 

For the various requirements, you will always find the suitable combination of measuring device, sensor and measuring software. With the wide range of basic instruments in combination with more than 60 CASSY sensors and the CASSY Lab 2 software, the CASSY App, the innovative CASSY.App Web for the Mobile-CASSY 2 WiFi or the digital experiment instructions Lab Docs, CASSY is convincing in schools, vocational training colleges and universities all over the world.

Even today, the sensors from the first hour can still be used without any problems. However, it is not only the durability of the CASSY products, but also the didactic concept, the intuitive handling and the high technical standard that make CASSY unique.

For every teaching situation ...

CASSY provides the perfect solution. Whether you want to record the measured values from the experiments with or without a computer or also include tablets in digital lessons, CASSY is developed for every need and can be flexibly expanded. You will find the suitable combination of measuring device, sensor and measuring software for the different requirements resulting from a demonstration or a student experiment as well as a practical training.

The wide range of measuring instruments ...

Choose from a wide range of measuring instruments for your lessons. Our product portfolio ranges from the award-winning Sensor-CASSY, which is particularly suitable for demonstration experiments, to the incomparable student measuring instrument Mobile-CASSY 2 WiFi, to our Power-CASSY, CASSY-Display, and the Universal Measuring Instruments.

over 60 cassy Sensors ...

In combination with over 60 CASSY sensors, which can be used compatibly across the CASSY system, the measurement data acquisition of more than 110 measured quantities is possible. The sensors are automatically detected, making time-consuming configuration unnecessary. Fast and precise measurement data acquisition is a feature of all our CASSY measuring devices.

CASSY LAB 2 ...

In conjunction with the CASSY Lab 2 software, CASSY has become one of the most successful and popular solutions for measured data acquisition and evaluation. With CASSY Lab 2, the acquired measurements can be interpreted and evaluated in detail. You can use the repared settings for this or generate your own configurations. The educational concept and intuitive operability of CASSY have been impressive to both schools and universities throughout the world.

Developed in close cooperation with teachers and professors, the CASSY measuring instruments, CASSY sensors and CASSY software are characterised by their ease of handling and maximum practical relevance. Ultimately, CASSY enables long-lasting and sucessful learning.

CASSY App ...

For digital education in the natural sciences, the CASSY app complements the portfolio. With it, students and teachers or instructors can integrate tablets and smartphones for digital experimentation in student and demonstration experiments.

Innovative CASSY.App WEB
for Mobile-CASSY 2 Wifi ...

Open experimentation in student groups with tablet & co. made simple.

Up to 4 students can connect to one Mobile-CASSY 2 WiFi at the same time. The CASSY.App Web does not have to be installed on the devices because it runs as a web app in the browser.

Detailed experiment instructions ...

We offer more than 400 sets of detailed experiment instructions for demonstration experiments and over 1,500 for student experiments. You can use them to support preparation and for carrying out and supplementing experiments.
