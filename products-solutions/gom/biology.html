Biology

Knowledge in such fields as ecology, genetics and neurophysiology is growing at an exponential rate. This knowledge has long since found its way into modern medical treatments, the cultivation of new, genetically modified plants, ecosystem conservation and the development of vaccines. The technical and scientific implications of these developments are extremely complex and appear to be only comprehensible to specialists.
 
LD DIDACTIC has committed itself to making these complex topics more clearly understandable and exploring nature and the environment in the spirit of “experimentation first” and “book-knowledge” second.

  • Demonstration experiments and practicals
  • University training and education
