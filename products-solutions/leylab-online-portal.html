TIME-SAVING PREPARATION - ALWAYS & EVERYWHERE

You want to prepare the experiments effectively and flexibly for your lessons in the collection, the staffroom or at home on your laptop?

You want to look in front of your equipment cabinet on your smartphone to see which devices you need for an experiment and where they are?


Our solution for you: LeyLab.

LeyLab is a modern tool for organizing and inventorying experiments and devices.

With LeyLab, your institute can record device types and their storage locations as part of your equipment inventory. LeyLab can also manage the same device across different storage locations and handle loaned-out devices. This provides an up-to-date overview of the collection for all colleagues.

Another highlight of LeyLab is the experiment management feature. It suggests topic-specific experiments from the LD experiment library (based on available equipment) to teachers. Additionally, you can add your own experiments or supplement LD experiments with pictures or notes.

Inventory & organisation of experiments & devices

EXPERIMENT COLLECTION

In the new LeyLab online portal, teachers can quickly and easily access their own selection of experiments and all LD experiments from anywhere. This selection can be supplemented with additional information, such as their own documents or videos.

To ensure a simple overview, the experiments are sorted according to category and topic. Specific searches can be made with the built-in filter function. The detailed list of devices helps to immediately identify which devices are required for the experiment and which are available on-site.

Teachers can also save their own brand-new experiments in LeyLab. Additional information, such as safety data sheets, can be added to these experiments.

DEVICE COLLECTION

Adding LD DIDACTIC products from the integrated device library is user-friendly and requires just a click of the mouse. Information such as storage location or comments can be easily added. Furthermore, the technical data of the device, as well as documents and possible experiments, can be viewed immediately. The filter function also facilitates ease of use and provides an exact search result.

Devices from other manufacturers can also be entered, ensuring that the system can always provide an up-to-date overview of the entire collection of teaching materials.
